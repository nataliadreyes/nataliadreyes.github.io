Apropos Natalia

I'm Natalia. At UC Berkeley, I study Rhetoric with a concentration in Public Discourse; I also read and practice literary writing. I am currently writing a thesis on the rhetoric of Chicano literary criticism.

I enjoy reading, writing, taking photos, cooking, and doing nothing.

This is my first website.
