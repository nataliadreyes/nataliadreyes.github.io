Apropos Natalia

Generally, I answer to Natalia. At UC Berkeley, I study Rhetoric with a concentration in Public Discourse; I also read and practice literary writing. I am currently writing a thesis on the rhetoric of Chicano literary criticism.

I enjoy reading, writing, taking photos, cooking, and doing nothing.

This is my first website. There aren't any flying monkeys or whimsical animations. I'm sure you can find them elsewhere.

This site will grow. The negative space is only such if you have a negative attitude.
